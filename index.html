about

I am a college student studying Graphic Design. I have been creating professionally since 2020, working with a variety of programs, for personal and commercial projects. I strive for amazing results, scalability, accessibility, and beauty in all the work I make. I am from Chicago, and I am in the CST timezone.

process

I tend to work in a circular process of research, drafting, rough composition, and finalization. I always research and take inspiration from reputable sources along with my client, and I continually communicate throughout the process. I am able to develop many features and functionalities myself, but I am also experienced in a variety of frameworks and libraries should they be preferred. I often times strongly recommend that certain components should make use of third-party, actively maintained libraries for security or performance reasons.
